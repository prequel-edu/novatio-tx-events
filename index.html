What is Novatio?

A school built around your child — not the other way around.

Novatio is a private, virtual, AI-driven school for grades 4–8. Students master core academics in just 2 hours each morning, then spend their afternoons on real-world skills and projects.

No academic requirements. No waitlist. No admissions test. Every family is welcome.

✅ TEFA covers 100% of tuition

Texas families can use their TEFA funds ($10,474) to cover Novatio's $8,500 tuition — with approximately $2,000 left over for other educational expenses on the Odyssey marketplace.

Cognia Accredited Novatio is fully accredited by Cognia, ensuring your child receives a recognized, high-quality education.
🤖

AI-Powered Morning Academics

2 focused hours of personalized learning — adapts to exactly where your child is. Students learn up to 4.5× faster than in traditional classrooms.

🎯

Afternoon Clubs & Real-World Skills

Goal setting, coding, financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and more. Life skills built into the school day — no extra programs needed.

👤

A Dedicated Mentor Guide

Every student is paired with a real teacher who provides daily coaching, oversight, and support. Your child is truly seen and known.

🏫

Social & Collaborative

Students work on group projects, build friendships, and connect with peers — all within the virtual school environment.
